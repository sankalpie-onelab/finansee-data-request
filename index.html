Finansee Data Deletion Request

To request deletion of your account and associated data from Finansee, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to info@finansee.eu with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your account email address or user ID in the message
  3. We will process your request within 30 days
Send Deletion Request

What We Delete

  • Your account information (email, name)
  • All app usage data
  • Any stored financial information

What We Keep

  • Transaction records required by financial regulations (for 5 years)
  • Anonymous usage statistics that cannot be tied to your identity

For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
